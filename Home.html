We're thrilled to be able announce the details of the 6th Atlantic Paddle Symposium.

Our 2013 event will bring us back to New Brunswick and will be centered in historic Saint John.

Saint John gives us easy access to the Bay Of Fundy, the Reversing Falls, as well countless rivers, creeks and lakes. We'll have the variety of locations needed for all paddlers - novice, intermediate and advanced to develop, enhance, expand and challenge their skills.

A big thank you to all of you who attended the event in the past, and a big welcome to all newcomers. 

 
Contact the Atlantic Paddling Symposium if you'd like to coach in 2013, or if you have suggestions for sessions.

The Symposium is here for the benefit of both paddlers and paddling professionals. If you are a Paddle Canada certified instructor or guide and you'd like to volunteer, please contact us. We'd love to hear from you.

Thanks to our sponsors! We couldn't do this without their generous help!
