The Bay of Fundy Sea Kayaking Symposium is a 3 day paddling event that combines world class coaching with paddlers in one of the most spectacular coastal setting in Eastern North America. The Tusket islands are not only rich with history and Acadian culture they boast large tides as the Bay of Fundy is home to the largest tidal range in the world. The area provides opportunities to paddle great rock gardens, tide currents and surfing is only a short drive from the base at Ye Olde Argyler Lodge.
