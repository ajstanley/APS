About the Atlantic Paddling Symposium

The first Atlantic Paddling Symposium was held in Port Mouton Nova Scotia in 2006 as way for paddling professionals to get to know each other and share their paddling and coaching techniques. By the end of the weekend we'd decide this had to be an annual event, with offerings for paddlers and paddling professionals of all levels.

Each year the event is hosted by the paddling community of a different Atlantic Province. Each new venue gives us a chance to explore new waters and new coastlines with new companions.

This year's venue promises an amazingly wide variety of paddling experience. We have white water, flat water, the Reversing Fall and exciting coastal waters. You will have the opportunity to expand your experience by trying new techniques in new conditions with some of the best paddling coaches in Canada.
