Jacqueline Holmes Sea Kayak Coach (Cape Breton)

Jacqueline Holmes grew up canoeing London, Ontario's Thames River with her father and has paddled in dugout canoes in South America and Haida Gwaii. She has led sea kayaking trips and instruction in BC, Baja and Atlantic Canada and has hung out with dolphins and manatees by stand-up paddleboard. Paddle with Jacqueline and you'll talk, laugh and smile your way through anything.
