Courses before and after the Symposium

Every year we have paddlers and instructors coming from farther and farther away to experience the amazing paddling we have here in the Atlantic provinces. Each year we try to take advantage of this gathering to offer courses before and after the Symposium allowing participants additional access to the coaching staff and conditions they've travelled so far to enjoy.

There are two courses offered this year so far. If your organization plans to offer courses in this time period, please let us know. We'd love to have your information up here as well.


Chris Lockyer and his team will be presenting an Advanced Coastal Paddling course from April 29th to May 4th with guest coach Andy Stamp, Level 5 BCU Coach.

Participants will focus on the skills necessary for leading and managing groups in advanced sea states and tidal conditions. You will be exposed to no landing zones, surf beaches, and rescues in advanced conditions.

Immediately following the Symposium Chris will be offering a Level 2 Seakayaking Instructor's Course. Andy Stamp will be the featured guest Coach for this session as well.

This 5 day programs will expose you to the entire Level 2 skills curriculum in a challenging and creative learning environment.
